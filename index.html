About me

I'm currently pursuing my Master of Engineer in Computer Science from Cornell University at the Cornell Tech campus in NYC.

I love solving the challenges faced by data intensive applications. In this day and age that usually involves engineering a productionised application of machine learning. My favourite part of advanced analytics is taking the insights discovered by a data scientist and figuring out how to turn that into a workable product.

Graduated with a BSc in Biology from the University of Manchester in the UK. My capstone project was in computational biology, modelling evolutionary changes in viral protein structure. This began my shift into computer science and I have been head down learning everything I can since then!
